VISION

Our dreams

01

The Egg —
A Space for Sound, Silence & Ceremony

Event Space

In our park, there lies an old concrete water reservoir — 11 meters long and 4 meters deep. Its oval shape resembles an egg — a perfect symbol of birth, silence, and potential.

  • Breathtaking acoustics — a natural amplifier
  • Sound healing, meditation & ceremony
  • Natural amphitheater, up to 30 people
  • Sustainable creation — the structure already exists

02

Ceremonial Sauna & Ritual Zone

Purification & Connection

A natural wood-fired sauna inspired by North American and Slavic traditions of purification. Features relaxation area, showers, changing rooms, and a dedicated space for meditation or group processes.

03

Hospitality Zone

  • Neo-yurts and modular eco-homes
  • Designed for 30+ guests in comfort
  • Fully immersed in landscape, with minimal visual impact
  • Blends glamping, nature living, and creative residencies

04

Creative & Educational Infrastructure

  • Music and podcast studio
  • Outdoor amphitheater
  • Workshop spaces for artistic, movement, and educational programs
  • Residencies, talks, and cultural laboratories

05

Ecological & Regenerative

  • Permaculture gardens
  • Spiral's own honey production (Spiral Honey)
  • Fruit trees, herbs, edible and medicinal plants
  • A botanical living library curated over time

JOIN US

Let's Create the Future, Together

Our Design Philosophy

Deeply inspired by Christopher Alexander's vision of living architecture. Buildings are living forms that grow out of place, purpose, and human connection. Slow, intentional steps, listening to the land and its stories.

Join Our Vision

Spiral is a living, breathing vision — and we're inviting co-creators, not executors. We bring a clear concept, strong values, and a grounded step-by-step plan, but we're equally open to new ideas, bold inputs, and co-creative sparks.

We want to build Spiral with people who feel joy, challenge, and deep meaning in shaping a park of future culture — right here, in one of the most magnetic places on Earth. If you're not just designing buildings, but crafting experiences, rituals, and portals to what's next — let's talk.
