Why Spiral Park
Rare land asset with no direct competitor

2.4 hectares of hillside land within Funchal — 15 minutes from the city centre, in the forest — with confirmed tourism construction rights. Comparable parcels of this scale inside the city are effectively unavailable.

Madeira's premium experiential travel market is growing. The land is classified as "Espaços Urbanos de Expansão" (Urban Expansion Zone), with a 2026 PDM update expected to reclassify it to full Urban status — unlocking significantly higher construction capacity and a structural capital appreciation floor independent of operational performance. Downside is anchored by the land. Upside is driven by a scalable hospitality business with an established community.
