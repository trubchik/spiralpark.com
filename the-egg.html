SPIRAL PARK · MADEIRA

THE EGG

A Space for Sound, Silence & Ceremony

In our park, there lies an old concrete water reservoir — 11 meters long and 4 meters deep.
Once, it held more than 120 cubic meters of water, feeding fountains and irrigation systems.
For many years, it stood unused — a sleeping vessel.

Its oval shape resembles an egg —
a perfect symbol of birth, silence, and potential

And then we understood
what it truly was

The acoustics inside are breathtaking — a natural amplifier, resonating with warmth and depth.
We realized this is not an abandoned tank.
It's a ready-made sacred space for sound healing, meditation, and ceremony — a natural amphitheater that can host up to 30 people.

ART PERFORMANCE

SUSTAINABLE CREATION

Conscious use
of resources

This project perfectly reflects our philosophy of conscious and efficient use of resources.

We would hardly have dared to build a dedicated sound space from scratch — it would have been far too costly and complex. But life offered us a gift: the structure was already here — solid, resonant, and waiting to be awakened.

INVESTMENT NEEDED

€38,000

To adapt what already exists: installing circular skylights, creating a fireplace, adding a new side entrance and tunnel connection, and bringing in electricity.

That's all it takes to transform this place — to turn concrete into consciousness.
